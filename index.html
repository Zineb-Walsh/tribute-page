Loic Prigent's bio

Loic Prigent born in 1973, in Plouescat in Britany, is a french fashion journalist for 'Vogue'and 'Liberation', and founder of the Production company 'Deralf'. His love for writing began when he was only 12 and still a student in Highschool. At the age of 20, he moves to Paris, and starts writing for Liberation, about fashion and music. His Unfiltered opinion and criticism made his writings remarkable. But his success story started in 2005 when he produced a documentary about the Chanel house "Signé Chanel" on Arte, which allowed him to gain more notoriety.

In 2007,Loic starts his own production company, which will be followed by a fashion documentary series about the backstage and preparations of Prestigious Fashion brands. He worked with Jean Paul Gaultier, Versace... Recently Mr Prigent became one of the most famous fashion journalists on Youtube. On his Youtube Chanenel he documents the fashion week backstages in multiple location: New York, Paris as well as workshops and parades. And also interviews Runway Models and Designers.

In one of his interviews, Loic was asked about his opinion on Fashion, to which he replied: "Fashion is hysteria, the light, the futile, the meaning, the nonsense, the fury. to live, the unconsciousness of everything, selfishness, futility, consciousness of everything, constantly renewed energy, narration, forgetting and memory, it is a sacred motor and a sacred revealer."
